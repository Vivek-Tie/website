This channel addresses riding skills, reviews and perspectives critical to becoming a masterful motorcyclist with a slant to ADV (adventure) riders. Knowing I can only train a fraction of the number of riders in the world I share free lessons and advise gained over my 26 yrs as a professional motorcycle trainer. I have traveled and trained riders all over the world. I don't try to make riders safer I try to make each ride more enjoyable for you. I want to make each day a little brighter and each ride more valuable. Life is too short to waste and the time we have to ride is limited so let's make the most of every km/mile.
